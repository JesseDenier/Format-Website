How Format Group Can Help You

Founded in 2001 by former employees of a major educational publishing company, The Format Group is a full-service production house in Austin, Texas. We specialize in the education market and provide customers with effective solutions to their challenges, from pieces of projects to turnkey workflows. We offer decades of experience in all phases of teaching and training projects. We have experience in the K–12 and college markets, as well as extensive expertise in workforce and apprenticeship training materials.

What Sets Format Group Apart


Longevity Most of our staff have been with us for 10–20 years because of the positive, flexible, goal-oriented work environment. Our clients benefit from knowing they're working with someone who has experience and will be a long-standing point of contact.

Communication Weekly company-wide status meetings are held to discuss and remedy any issues in a team-based format as they arise.

Project Management We have developed several customized database solutions for tracking and managing projects. We use Slack for up-to-the-minute communications among our team, whether they’re on- or off-site.
